About

I’m a software developer, proudly graduated from The Tech Academy’s Software Developer Boot Camp with a true love for coding and tackling new challenges.

Originally from Argentina, technology has always been a huge part of my life. Before diving into software development, I worked as an EMT, where I learned how to stay calm and think quickly under pressure.

A little while ago, my husband Duncan and I decided we were ready for a change. We both wanted to pursue something that truly excited us, so we took the leap into software development. It’s been an amazing journey so far, and I’m excited to continue growing, learning, and working on meaningful projects.

I’m trained and experienced in the following web and programming languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, C#, and more. This website showcases some of my work, where I share the projects I’ve created for people and companies to see. Feel free to explore and reach out if you’re interested in collaborating!
